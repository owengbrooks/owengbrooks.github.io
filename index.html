I am an economics undergraduate at the University of Oregon. My research is in international trade and economic history. I have a particular interest in global value chains, economic geography, and the political economy of trade policy. I am working towards attaining a PhD in economics.

My current research project examines the welfare and sectoral price effects of the McKinley Tariff using a multi-sector input-output framework and production data from the 1890 Census of Manufacturing. I am interested in understanding how high tariffs affect effect different sectors through input-output linkages. You can read the abstract for the project here
